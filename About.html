To Book Your Tickets Website

BookYourTicket.com is an online movie ticketing website founded by Ramandeep Kaur and Bollywood.com in 2018; CBS Corporation, Famous Players, and National Amusements all came on board prior to launch; and it is now a subsidiary of Fandango Media. BookYourTicket.com provides movie times for all theaters, and online ticket purchasing for all Clearview Cinemas and National Amusements theaters, among other smaller chains; such as Mann Theatres in India. In 2019, BookYourTicket.com sold over 16 million tickets for over 200 exhibitors, with 14,000 screens.
